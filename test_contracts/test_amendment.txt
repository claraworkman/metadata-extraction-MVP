AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Amendment No. 1 ("Amendment") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, by and between:

Circle K Polska Sp. z o.o. (Internal Entity)
and
Polski Dostawca Żywności Sp. z o.o. (Supplier)

WHEREAS, the parties entered into a Master Supply Agreement dated April 1, 2024 ("Master Agreement");

WHEREAS, the parties wish to amend certain terms of the Master Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. REFERENCE TO MASTER AGREEMENT
This Amendment modifies the Master Supply Agreement dated 04/01/2024 between Circle K Polska and Polski Dostawca Żywności.

2. EXTENSION OF TERM
The expiration date of the Master Agreement is hereby extended from March 31, 2026 to December 31, 2026.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment terms are modified from Net 45 to Net 60 days.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Amendment shall be governed by the laws of Poland.

5. EFFECTIVE DATE
This Amendment is effective as of January 15, 2025.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Amendment.

Circle K Polska Sp. z o.o.
By: Jan Kowalski, Legal Director

Polski Dostawca Żywności Sp. z o.o.
By: Maria Nowak, CEO
