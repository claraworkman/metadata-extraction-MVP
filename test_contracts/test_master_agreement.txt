MASTER SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Master Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2024, by and between:

Circle K Polska Sp. z o.o., with registered office in Warsaw, Poland (hereinafter "Circle K" or "Customer")

and

Polski Dostawca Żywności Sp. z o.o., with registered office in Krakow, Poland (hereinafter "Supplier")

RECITALS

Circle K operates convenience stores throughout Poland and requires a reliable supplier of food and beverage products.

Supplier has the capacity and expertise to provide such products.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - SCOPE OF SERVICES
Supplier shall provide food and beverage products including snacks, drinks, and packaged meals for Circle K convenience stores across Poland.

ARTICLE 2 - TERM
This Agreement shall commence on April 1, 2024 and shall continue for a period of 24 months, expiring on March 31, 2026. The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 60 days written notice of non-renewal.

ARTICLE 3 - PAYMENT TERMS
Payment shall be made within 45 days of invoice date (Net 45).

ARTICLE 4 - CONTRACT VALUE
The total estimated annual value is 2,500,000 PLN.

ARTICLE 5 - GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Poland.

ARTICLE 6 - TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by providing 90 days written notice to the other party.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
